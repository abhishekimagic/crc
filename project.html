New-age Elegance

Classics are never passé. The new-age lifestyle cum commercial destination is a true reflection of luxury and contemporariness and the spectacular confluence of environment and remarkable aesthetics.

The 1.1 million square feet of state-of-the-art, futuristic and elegant technology tower has a campus feel, retail spaces and 5-star hotel serviced suites which will be the country’s most iconic lifestyle cum commercial destination.

Built sustainably, the technology towers and the office spaces are iconic and world-class. The events and social plazas are the symbols of energy and vibrancy.
